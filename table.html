Study With TENSE
TENSE
PRESENT Indefinite sub+v1+obj I am a Student He goes to School I teach in a School
Continuous sub+am/is/are+verb+ing+obj I am reading She is making tea They are playing card
Perfect Sub+have/has+v3+obj I have eaten rice He has gone to School They have done their homework
Perfect Continuous sub+have been/has been+verb+ing+obj Mr Amin has been living in Dhaka They have been playing cricket for two hours Sajal has been working since Morning
PAST Indefinite Sub+v2+obj Raju was ill He went to School yesterday I ate rice
Continuous Sub+was/were+verb+ing+obj I was reading People were waiting for him We were playing games in the school playground
Perfect Sub+had+v3+obj I had done the work She had sung the song The train had left before I reached the station
Perfect Continuous Sub+had been+verb+ing+obj They had been waiting for a long time
FUTURE Indefinite Sub+shall/will+v1+obj He will do the work I shall go to school
Continuous Sub+shall be/will be+verb+ing+obj She will be cooking for us
Perfect Sub+Shall have/Will have+v3+obj He will have done the sum
Perfect Continuous Sub+Shall have been/will have been+verb+ing+obj I shall have been reading the book
